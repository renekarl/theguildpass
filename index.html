FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

It's simple: Browse deals in the app, tap "Redeem" when you're ready to pay, enter your PIN, and hand your phone to the staff. They enter their PIN to verify, and your discount is applied. The whole process takes seconds.

We're launching in Tallinn, Estonia first. We're actively signing up restaurants, hotels, and spas. After Tallinn, we plan to expand to Riga and Vilnius.

We'll offer 2-for-1 main courses, percentage discounts (up to 50% off), fixed amount savings, free items, and exclusive special offers. Each venue sets their own deals.

We use a dual-PIN system: you enter your personal PIN, then the staff enters the restaurant's PIN. This industry-standard approach prevents fraud and ensures only valid redemptions. No special hardware needed.

Soon! Join the waitlist to be notified the moment we go live. Founding members will get exclusive early access and special perks.

With a paid membership, you'll get unlimited redemptions. Some individual deals may have daily limits (e.g., one 2-for-1 per visit), but there's no cap on total usage.
