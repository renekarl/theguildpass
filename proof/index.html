FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

It's simple: Browse deals in the app, tap "Redeem" when you're ready to pay, enter your PIN, and hand your phone to the staff. They enter their PIN to verify, and your discount is applied. The whole process takes seconds.

We're launching in Tallinn, Estonia with 100+ partner venues including restaurants, hotels, and spas. We're expanding to Riga and Vilnius soon, with plans to cover all three Baltic capitals.

We offer 2-for-1 main courses, percentage discounts (up to 50% off), fixed amount savings, free items, and exclusive special offers. Each venue sets their own deals, so there's always something new to discover.

We use a dual-PIN system: you enter your personal PIN, then the staff enters the restaurant's PIN. This industry-standard approach prevents fraud and ensures only valid redemptions. No special hardware or training needed for venues.

Yes, you can cancel anytime from the app settings. If you cancel within your 7-day free trial, you won't be charged. After that, your membership remains active until the end of your billing period.

With a paid membership, you get unlimited redemptions. Some individual deals may have daily limits (e.g., one 2-for-1 per visit), but there's no cap on total usage. Use it every day if you want!
